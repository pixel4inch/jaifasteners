About Us

Jai Fasteners Private Ltd., is one of the leading and fastest growing manufacturers of Fasteners in India. Established in 2004, with ISO 9001-2015 & CE Certification, the company caters its products to several Industries.

Currently, we are into the manufacture of Industrial and Automobile Fasteners, High Tensile Fasteners, Brass and Copper Metal Parts for Transformers, Switchgears, and Power Sector Needs. We also manufacture and supply Sag Rods, Anchor Bolts, HSS Bolts, and MS Bolts for Pre-Fabricated Building Systems.

Our Manufacturing Unit, centrally located in Patancheru, Hyderabad, is out- sized and large operational vicinity, equipped with latest Machinery, and Testing & Laboratory Equipment.

Our Unit is approved with M/S National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC), Power Grid, National Hydraulic Power Corporation, Indian Railways, Integral Coach Factory (Chennai), and Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd.

We employ professionally skilled Engineers and experienced Supervisors to plug-in latest Technology.
